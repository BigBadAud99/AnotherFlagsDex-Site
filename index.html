The only Dex where it's about Custom Countries

AnotherFlagsDex is a custom Ballsdex Bot made by Dittor67
Here, instead of the recognisable flags you know, it's user made flags! Completely custom!
Some are redesigned version of real countries and some are new new countries
Catch, exchange, and battle the flags with your friends!

What are you waiting for? Invite the bot now by going to the Bot tab and click Invite Bot button!


Important notes ▼

1. Remember to send exactly the same file from the bot, not a screenshot

otherwise the result may be inconsistent.

2. Pasting a URL of a photo weighing more than 6MB will return nothing,

these are AWS limitations that can't be bypassed.

3. If the wrong answer is returned - the ball probably received a new art.
